Welcome to Gaoxiong Zeng's Homepage!

Gaoxiong Zeng (曾高雄 / Gordon) is a scientific researcher with Network Technology Lab of Huawei. He received his Ph.D. in computer science and engineering from Hong Kong University of Science and Technology in 2022, and his B.E. in electronic engineering from University of Science and Technology of China in 2015. He is broadly interested in computer networks and systems. His work has been published on various top venues such as SIGCOMM, NSDI, HPCA, INFOCOM, ICNP, IWQoS, and ToN, etc.
