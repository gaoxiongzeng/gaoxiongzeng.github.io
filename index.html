Welcome to Gaoxiong Zeng's Homepage!

Gaoxiong Zeng (曾高雄 / Gordon) is a researcher with Network Technology Lab of Huawei. He received his Ph.D. in computer science and engineering from Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, where he worked with Prof. Kai Chen. He is broadly interested in computer networks and systems. His work has been published on various top venues such as SIGCOMM, NSDI, INFOCOM, ICNP, and ToN, etc.
