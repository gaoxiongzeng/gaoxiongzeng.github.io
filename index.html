Gaoxiong Zeng (曾高雄) is a Ph.D. student in computer science at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), advised by Prof. Kai Chen. He obtained his B.E. degree in electronic engineering from University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in 2015.

His research work mainly focuses on data center networking, and machine learning systems.
