I am a Ph.D. student at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE), Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST). I am advised by Prof. Kai Chen. Before that, I obtained my B.E. degree in electronic engineering from the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in 2015.

My research work mainly focuses on data center networking and machine learning systems.
