Welcome to Gaoxiong Zeng's Homepage!

Gaoxiong Zeng is a 6th-year Ph.D. student in computer science at Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), advised by Prof. Kai Chen. He received his B.E. degree in electronic engineering from University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in 2015. His research areas of interest include computer networks and systems, with special focuses on data center networking and transport protocols.
