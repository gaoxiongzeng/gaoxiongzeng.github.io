Welcome to Gaoxiong Zeng's Homepage!

Gaoxiong Zeng is a staff researcher with Network Technology Lab of Huawei. He received his Ph.D. in computer science and engineering from Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, where he worked with Prof. Kai Chen. His research areas of interest include computer networks and systems, with special focuses on data center networking and transport protocols. His work has been published on various top venues such as SIGCOMM, INFOCOM, ICNP, and ToN, etc.
